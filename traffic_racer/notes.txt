??? Ham "$(function(){};" duoc goi khi nao?
Khi chuong trinh chay thi ngay lap tuc "$(function(){};" duoc chay va ham nay chi duoc chay duy nhat mot lan

??? "$(document).on()" duoc chay khi nao?
Ham nay chay lien tuc khi chay chuong trinh, no luon lang nghe cac event, moi khi co event vi du nhu "keyup" thi function xu ly se duoc goi

??? Hoat dong cua ham "requestAnimationFrame" la gi?
Ham nay se yeu cau browser repaint lai man hinh, sau do se thuc hien ham ben trong ma requestanimationframe goi den! 
Vi du: requestAnimationFrame(left) thi browser se repaint lai screen sau do thuc hien ham "left"
